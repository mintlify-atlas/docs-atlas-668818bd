mypos is a lightweight Minecraft Fabric mod for dedicated servers. Type `mypos` in chat and it gets replaced with your current coordinates — privately, or inline as part of a longer message. No commands, no configuration, no client mod required.

## Get started in 60 seconds

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the mod">
    Grab the latest `.jar` from the [GitHub releases page](https://github.com/allen-wang-2001/mypos/releases).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drop it in your mods folder">
    Copy the `.jar` into your server's `mods/` directory alongside Fabric Loader and Fabric API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start your server">
    Launch the server as normal. mypos registers automatically — no configuration needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type mypos in chat">
    Send `mypos` by itself for a private coordinate readout, or use it inline: `meet me at mypos`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  mypos is a **server-side only** mod. Players do not need to install anything on their client.
</Note>
